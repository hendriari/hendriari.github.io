Privacy
Policy

How Nirankara Studio collects, uses, and protects your information when you use AI SnapExplain.

🕐 Last updated: April 23, 2026
01 / Data Collection
01
Information We Collect
📧Account information: Email address and name when you register or sign in via Google OAuth or email/password.
🖼️Images you upload: Images or photos you choose from your device gallery for AI explanation. These are processed temporarily and are not stored on our servers.
📊Usage data: Number of AI requests made, subscription status, and quota usage.
📱Device information: Device ID used as a secondary signal to prevent quota abuse.
02 / Data Usage
02
How We Use Your Information
🤖To provide and improve the AI explanation service.
⚙️To manage your account, subscription, and daily usage quota.
✉️To send transactional emails (e.g., password reset, OTP).
📢To display relevant advertisements (free tier only, via Google AdMob).
🔍To monitor app stability via Firebase Crashlytics.

04 / Security & Retention
04
Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Images you upload are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored.

05
Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, your data will be deleted within 30 days.

05 / Your Rights
06
Your Rights
Access the personal data we hold about you.
Request correction of inaccurate data.
Request deletion of your account and associated data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us or use the in-app account deletion feature.

📧 support@aisnapexplain.online
06 / Additional Info
07
Children's Privacy

AI SnapExplain is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

08
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or in-app notification.
